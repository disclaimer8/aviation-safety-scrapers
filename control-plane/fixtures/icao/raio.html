Regional Accident and Incident Investigation Organizations (RAIOs) and Investigation Cooperation Mechanisms (ICMs)

This page is maintained by ICAO and lists Regional Accident and Incident Investigation Organizations (RAIOs) together with regional Investigation Cooperation Mechanisms (ICMs). Membership lists are indicative only.

Regional Accident and Incident Investigation Organizations (RAIOs)

BAGAIA — Banjul Accord Group Accident Investigation Agency

Region: Western Africa

Member States: Cabo Verde, Gambia, Ghana; Guinea, Liberia; Nigeria, Sierra Leone.

Website: https://www.bagaia.org

IAC — Interstate Aviation Committee

Region: Commonwealth of Independent States

Member States: Russian Federation; Armenia; Azerbaijan; Belarus; Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Tajikistan; Turkmenistan.

Website: https://mak-iac.org

Investigation Cooperation Mechanisms (ICMs)

ARCM-MENA — Arab Regional Cooperation Mechanism (Middle East and North Africa)

Region: Middle East / North Africa

Participating States: Egypt, Jordan, Morocco; Saudi Arabia, United Arab Emirates.

Website: https://www.icao.int/MID/Pages/ARCM.aspx

ARCM-SAM — Regional Cooperation Mechanism for South America

Region: South America

Participating States: Argentina, Brazil; Chile, Colombia (observer: Panama; observers: Mexico).

Website: https://www.icao.int/SAM/Pages/default.aspx

ENCASIA — Eastern and Northern CIS Aviation Safety Initiative

Region: Eastern and Northern CIS

Participating States: Georgia, Republic of Moldova; Ukraine.

Website: https://www.icao.int/safety/Pages/ENCASIA.aspx

GRIAA — Gulf Region Inter-Authority Cooperation on Accident Investigation

Region: Gulf Region

Cooperating States: Bahrain, Kuwait; Oman, Qatar (observers: Yemen, Iraq).

Website: https://www.icao.int/MID/Pages/GRIAA.aspx
